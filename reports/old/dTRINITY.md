# dTRINITY

- the protocol looks a bit complex and using forks of multiple protocols
- dUSD token is upgradable and managed by multisig 2/3 without any timelock
- other contracts of the protocol are also managed by the same multisig.
- there is no bug bounty: https://docs.dtrinity.org/developer-guide/bug-bounty
- audits seems to cover only parts of the protocol: https://docs.dtrinity.org/developer-guide/audits-and-security
- TVL is really low: 3.5M: https://defillama.com/protocol/dtrinity
